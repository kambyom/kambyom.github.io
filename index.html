Building the resilience needed to navigate change in an uncertain future

Background

The planetary boundaries serve as a stark reminder that our world is changing rapidly. As we continue to push these boundaries, we must be prepared for the challenges that lie ahead, including climate change, biodiversity loss, soil erosion, and water scarcity.

By preparing for and adapting to these changes, we can mitigate the potential impacts and secure a sustainable future for ourselves and future generations.

What we do

We gather and build information technology tools to help individuals and organizations develop resilience. This includes educational content, apps that help people organize and relevant open-source software.

We also write sustainable custom software for nonprofit organizations and essential businesses.
If you need help building something, contact us.
